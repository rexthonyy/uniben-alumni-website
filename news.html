THE ANNUAL LECTURE / AWARDS / FUNDRAISING FOR ALUMNI SECRETARIAT BY THE UNIBEN ALUMNI ASSOCIATION, ASABA BRANCH

We wish to convey to you the compliments and good wishes of the University of Benin Alumni Association, Asaba Branch and to notify you that the Association’s Annual Lecture is scheduled to hold on Wednesday 16th December, 2020 at David’s Place, Anwai Road, Asaba by 5pm. This year’s lecture with the title, Emerging Trends in Africa Development: The Nigeria Experience” would be delivered by a business mogul, Dr. Gabriel Ogbechie; C.E.O. Rainoil Ltd.
